# ZenScape

**ZenScape** is a guided virtual reality experience designed to reduce anxiety, regulate breathing, and improve emotional awareness.  
The environment is intentionally slow, gentle, and non-competitive — there are **no enemies, timers, or fail states**.

---

## Features

### 1. Breathing Puzzle (Visual Biofeedback)
- A glowing sphere expands and contracts to guide inhale/exhale rhythm.
- The player mirrors the breathing pace.
- After several steady cycles, a soft chime or gentle light signals progress.

### 2. Emotion Identification Puzzle
- Emotion words (e.g., *Anxiety, Tension, Fear, Calm*) appear on a wall.
- The player selects the word that reflects their current emotional state.
- Short reflection cue appears:  
  *“Notice how this emotion feels in your body.”*

### 3. Cognitive Reframing Puzzle (Positive Thought Placement)
- Three objects are labeled: **Negative Thought**, **Neutral Thought**, **Positive Thought**.
- The player picks up the **Positive Thought** object and places it on a pedestal.
- The pedestal glows and a door slowly unlocks, signaling completion.
